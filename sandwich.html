Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread (white or brown)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 boiled potato, sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 small onion, thinly sliced
  • Green chutney or mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread butter on all bread slices.
  2. Apply green chutney or mayo on one side of each slice.
  3. Layer sliced vegetables on two slices of bread.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the veggies.
  5. Cover with the other two slices to make a sandwich.
  6. Optional: Grill or toast until golden brown.
  7. Cut and serve with ketchup or chutney.
