Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (penne, spaghetti or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and onions. Saut√© until soft.
  3. Add cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and toss well to coat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper as needed.
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot.
