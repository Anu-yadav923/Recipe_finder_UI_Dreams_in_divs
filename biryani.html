Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cleaned and cut
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Cook it 70% and set aside.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, salt, and ginger-garlic paste. Rest for 1 hour.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil/ghee. Add whole spices, then sliced onions. Fry until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add the marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  5. In a deep pot, layer chicken masala and partially cooked rice. Top each layer with mint, coriander, fried onions, and saffron milk.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
  7. Gently fluff and serve hot with raita or salad.
