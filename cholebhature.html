Ingredients for Chole (Chickpeas Curry):

  • 1 cup chickpeas (chole)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Ingredients for Bhature:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight. Pressure cook until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin, then saut√© onions and ginger-garlic paste until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree and all spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas and water. Simmer for 15‚Äì20 minutes. Garnish with coriander.
  5. For bhature: Mix flour, sooji, curd, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Knead into a soft dough.
  6. Cover and rest for 2 hours. Divide and roll into medium-sized rounds.
  7. Heat oil and deep fry until golden and puffed.
  8. Serve hot bhature with spicy chole and onion salad.
