Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for soaking and grinding)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them to a smooth batter using water. The consistency should be pourable.
  3. Let the batter ferment overnight or 8‚Äì10 hours in a warm place.
  4. Add salt and mix the batter well before cooking.
  5. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle and pour a ladleful of batter.
  6. Spread it in a circular motion to form a thin crepe.
  7. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  8. Cook until the bottom is golden brown. Fold and serve hot.
  9. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
