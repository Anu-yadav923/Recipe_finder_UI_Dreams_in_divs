Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Cold water (for soaking)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or herbs for seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin sticks.
  2. Soak them in cold water for at least 30 minutes (removes excess starch).
  3. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with a towel.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan on medium heat.
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches until they are soft but not brown (about 3-4 minutes). Remove and drain.
  6. Let them cool, then fry again on high heat until golden and crispy (2-3 minutes).
  7. Drain on paper towels, sprinkle with salt or seasonings, and serve hot!
