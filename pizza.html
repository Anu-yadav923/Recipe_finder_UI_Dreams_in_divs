Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (onion, capsicum, mushrooms, olives, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220¬∞C (425¬∞F).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  3. Add warm water and olive oil. Mix and knead into a soft dough.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough into a circle on a floured surface.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
  7. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  9. Slice and serve hot!
